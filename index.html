About

Lume & Fade is a game that brings the player into an immersive experience where they can play as Cur along the side of their companion, Rynn, and explore a world full of creatures and magical gems. A long lost friendship will be renewed, switching between alternative worlds will be possible, and collecting mysteriously powerful gems as a means to find their way home will seem to be the only hope before time runs out.

Tofu Game's Lume & Fade was developed over the span of 10 weeks for the project class INF 125/CS 113. We hope that you'll enjoy this game as much as we did creating it.
