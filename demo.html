Game Play

Controls

Use 'wasd' to attack, jump or switch between worlds or plug in a usb controller to do the same.

Key Features

  • Original artwork, storyline, characters and sound
  • Switch between two worlds
  • Attack enemies and complete tasks to solve environmental puzzles in order to progress through levels.
  • Collection of gems
  • Side-scrolling exploration

Level Design

There are a total of 3 levels in the game of Lume and Fade. The design and layout of each level will be unique with different enviornmental obstacles and changes that represent the progress through the storyline. Each level will utilize the switching mechanic and require different skills and strategy to complete. At the end of each level, a unique boss with special abilities must be defeated. All levels will also contain a total of 100 gems to collect. Each boss will hold 50 gems, while the other 50 will be scattered throughout the course.
